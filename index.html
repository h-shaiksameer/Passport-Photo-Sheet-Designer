Quick printing guide

Upload: use drag and drop, file picker, or Ctrl+V.

Print: use Actual Size / 100% for passport photos.

Export: PNG, JPEG, PDF, and SVG are generated fully in-browser.

Indian passport recommendation: 35 × 45 mm on 4×6 glossy paper, usually 8 photos per sheet.

A4 vs 4×6: same photo size, but A4 leaves extra white space.

About

Offline, frontend-only, production-ready

PhotoPrint Studio Pro runs entirely inside the browser with canvas-based rendering, high-DPI export, and print-aware CSS.

This build is static and deployable to Netlify, Vercel, or GitHub Pages without configuration.

Printing Guide

Recommended settings for passport sheets

Indian Passport Photos

Photo size: 35 × 45 mm

Recommended paper: 4×6 glossy photo paper (4R)

Typical output: 8 passport photos per sheet

Printing Advice

Use Actual Size / Scale 100%

Do not use Fit to Page for passport photos

Extra white space on A4 is expected
